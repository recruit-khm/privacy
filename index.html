Privacy Policy

Application: Odoo Messaging Integration
Operator: Khmelnitskiy Territorial Recruitment Support Center
Last updated: February 24, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Khmelnitskiy Territorial Recruitment Support Center ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data processed through the Odoo Messaging Integration application. This application integrates with the WhatsApp Business API (provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.) to facilitate recruitment communications and candidate management within our internal HR management system (Odoo).

2. Data We Collect

Through the WhatsApp Business API integration, we may process the following categories of personal data:

  • Phone numbers of individuals who initiate or receive contact via WhatsApp
  • Names and contact details voluntarily provided during recruitment communications
  • Message content related to recruitment inquiries and candidate applications
  • Recruitment pipeline and application status data stored in our CRM

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Personal data is collected and processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Conducting recruitment communications with candidates for military service
  • Managing recruitment workflows within our internal HR system
  • Responding to inquiries from individuals interested in military service positions
  • Ensuring compliance with Ukrainian legislation governing military recruitment

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent of the individual when they initiate or agree to contact via WhatsApp
  • Legitimate interest in conducting lawful military recruitment operations
  • Compliance with applicable Ukrainian law, including legislation governing the Armed Forces of Ukraine and mobilization procedures

5. Data Storage and Security

All personal data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Amazon Web Services) with strict access controls. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss. Access to recruitment data is restricted exclusively to authorized personnel involved in the recruitment process.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — as a technical necessity for WhatsApp Business API operation, subject to Meta's own Privacy Policy
  • Internal staff of Khmelnitskiy Territorial Recruitment Support Center directly involved in the recruitment process
  • State authorities of Ukraine where required by law

7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained for the period necessary to complete the recruitment process and as required by applicable Ukrainian legislation. Upon expiry of the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights

Individuals whose data we process have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal data held by us
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of their data, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
